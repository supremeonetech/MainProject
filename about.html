About WisdomCyber Innovations

WisdomCyber Innovations is a student-focused initiative offering workshops, templates, and hands-on projects that teach web development and cybersecurity fundamentals. This site is a final project for the WDD course and demonstrates responsive design, accessibility, and JavaScript-driven interactivity.

Our mission

To provide practical, accessible resources for students learning modern web development. We emphasize usable code, ethical cybersecurity practices, and community support.
